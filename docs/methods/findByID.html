Partial of findBy

Used to find one or more matching records by ID.

For instance if you have a RecordArray of students and you want to find out how many are living in the USA:

	// "students" is a RecordArray of students' information.
	const students1n2 = students.findByID([1,2]).listValues(name);
	console.log(`Student 1 and 2 are ${students1n2.join(' & ')}.`);

It is more likely that you will have a field in a record that is an ID of the record in another RecordArray

Partial of findBy

Used to find all matching records by indexing each record by the tag field.

For instance if you have a RecordArray of students and you want a count of those living in USA or Canada:

	// "students" is a RecordArray of students' information.
	const studentsUSA_CA = students.findByTag(['USA','Canada']).listValues(name);
	console.log(`Number of Students from USA and Canada are ${studentsUSA_CA.listValues('name').length}.`);
